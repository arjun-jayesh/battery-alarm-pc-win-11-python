## Extend Your Battery's Life, Effortlessly.

Did you know that keeping your laptop constantly plugged in at 100% degrades the battery's maximum capacity over time? The optimal zone for lithium-ion longevity is between **20% and 80%**.

**Battery Alarm** is a lightweight, zero-distraction utilitarian app designed to protect your battery hardware. It runs silently in the background and politely notifies you the exact moment your laptop reaches your optimal charge limit.

---

## Feature Highlights

### 🎯 Precision Targeting
You're in control. Use the sleek, modern slider to set your ideal charge target anywhere from **50% to 100%**. The app's beautiful, dynamic circular gauge updates in real-time, changing from red to amber to green based on your battery's health zone.

### 🛑 Intelligent "Auto-Stop"
No need to scramble to turn off the alarm. The moment you physically unplug your laptop charger from the wall or the device, **Battery Alarm intelligently detects the power state change and immediately silences the alarm.**

### 🖥️ "Float" Desktop Widget
Keep an eye on your charge without opening any windows. Battery Alarm includes a gorgeous, frameless, and completely transparent mini-widget that sits neatly on your desktop. 
- **Draggable:** Just click and drag it safely into any corner of your screen.
- **Unobtrusive:** It sits *behind* your open applications, acting like part of your wallpaper.
- **Interactive:** A single click instantly opens the main dashboard.

### 🚀 Zero-Friction Startup
Protecting your battery shouldn't be a chore you have to remember every day. With a single click of the **"Run on Startup"** toggle, Battery Alarm embeds itself natively into Windows. The minute you turn on your PC, it’s already standing by in the system tray. 

### 🌙 Built for Windows 11
Say goodbye to outdated, clunky utility apps. Battery Alarm features a stunning Dark Mode aesthetic crafted specifically for the Windows 11 design language. Enjoy soft drop-shadows, perfectly rounded corners, high-contrast typography, and a native fluidity.

### 😴 Snooze & Sleep
In the middle of a meeting when the alarm rings? Hit the **Snooze** button for exactly 5 minutes of silence, or simply click **Stop** if you want to leave it charging to 100% just this once.

## Unseen & Out of Your Way

We believe utilities should stay out of your taskbar. 
* Close the app window, and it gracefully minimizes into the System Tray.
* Hover over the tray icon for a quick read of your battery percentage.
* Right click the tray icon for instant access to hiding your widget or pausing the alarm entirely.

## Ready to protect your laptop?

Just double-click `BatteryAlarm.exe` to get started. No installation wizards, no bloated frameworks. Just a tiny, perfectly crafted tool that does exactly what it promises.
