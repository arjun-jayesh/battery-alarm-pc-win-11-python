⚡ Battery Alarm
Features Source Code
🔥 V1.0 Available for Windows 10/11

Stop slowly killing your laptop battery.

A gorgeous, zero-distraction desktop utility that alerts you exactly when your laptop hits its optimal charge limit. Open-source and free forever.

Download .exe (46MB) GitHub
🎯

Precision Targeting

Adjust your ideal charge limit anywhere from 50% to 100%. The app will silently wait until your exact threshold is met.

🛑

Intelligent Auto-Stop

The moment you physically unplug your laptop from the wall, the alarm instantly silences itself. No clicking required.

🖼️

Float Desktop Widget

A completely transparent, interactive, and beautifully understated widget that sits behind your apps right on your desktop wallpaper.
